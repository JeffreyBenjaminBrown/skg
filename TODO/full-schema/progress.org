** stage 1 : rename override relationship roles
*** global string replacement would be unsafe
The words "replacement" and "replaced" appear in many unrelated
contexts, including buffer/string replacement and content-link
replacement.  The implementation should classify matches before
editing rather than applying a mechanical global rename.
*** fixture IDs may contain stale vocabulary
Some test fixture IDs appear to include old words such as
'overrides-view-of-1'.  These are IDs rather than role labels.
The implementation plan leaves room to keep them if renaming would
create noisy fixture churn.
