** What's done and went well
*** Step 1 (c94180e6) + Step 2 (25adab0e)
    Save-intention revert and dead-predicate removal (pre-this-session
    in the plan's numbering; committed earlier).
*** Step 3 (this commit): id-free retention
    Made InactiveNode IRRELEVANT in both content-reconcile
    ('update_buffer/reconcile/content.rs') and the partner-col path
    ('to_org/complete/partner_col/child_data.rs'). The reconciler
    preserves irrelevant children as-is, so a retained placeholder
    survives without a goal id. 'omit_inactive_members'
    ('update_buffer/reconcile/mod.rs') lost its 'retained' parameter
    (all 9 call sites updated) and 'retained_inactive_children' is
    deleted. Full Rust suite green (352 tests), including the
    source-switch retained case (source_sets::all_tests) and the
    omitted-inactive + weave case (partner_col_matrix).

*** Step 4 (this commit): drop the inactive->Deleted feature
    Deleted 'convert_inactive_to_deleted_if_deleted' and its BFS call
    ('update_buffer/complete.rs'); the inactive arm is now the shared
    no-op. When a save deletes an inactive node its anonymous
    placeholder just lingers until the next full rerender drops it,
    rather than flipping to a leaky "DELETED" marker. This reverses the
    earlier fix recorded in TODO/problems.org ("Inactive nodes are
    never converted to Vognode::Deleted") -- deliberately, since that
    conversion is a privacy leak and uninteresting to a user who hid
    the source. Removed its unit test ('tests/unit/complete.rs', whose
    only test was for this feature) and the '#[path]' mod that included
    it. Full suite green (443).

*** Step 5 (this commit): drop inactive from collateral
    'pids_from_viewforest' ('types/views_state.rs') now collects only
    Active vognodes. An inactive placeholder is anonymous, so a save
    touching its node need not re-render views that show it; its active
    descendants register themselves. Updated the 'ViewState.pids' doc.
    Full suite green (443).

*** Step 6 (this commit): delete dead inactive arms in diff render
    Removed the two 'Vognode::Inactive' arms in 'to_org/render/diff.rs'
    (membership stamping + removed-member phantom anchoring). They are
    unreachable: diff mode requires the "all" source set, under which
    nothing is inactive. Full suite green (443).

*** Step 7 (this commit): anonymize the wire + strip the struct
    'InactiveNode' is now a fieldless unit struct with a doc comment
    recording the de-novo-scope fact Jeff asked for. Ripples handled:
    - 'mk_inactive_viewnode ()' takes no args; all 4 call sites
      (source_switch, source_sets, to_org/util, reconcile/content)
      updated; the source_sets 'Treatment::Convert' lost its payload.
    - render: 'org_to_text' emits bare '(inactiveNode)'.
    - parse: '(inactiveNode)' parses with no required fields; legacy
      field-bearing forms are tolerated and discarded (stale buffers
      still round-trip). Dropped the 'inactive_membership' /
      'inactive_overridesHere' ViewnodeMetadata fields.
    - 'Vognode::id' now returns 'Option<&ID>' (None for inactive) and
      'pid_and_source' returns None for inactive; callers updated
      ('id_if_vognode', 'get_id_from_treenode',
      'viewnode_nodecomplete', the ancestor pid/source readers Err on
      an inactive ancestor, which is correct).
    - deleted 'InactiveNode::collected_id'; inactive arms removed from
      content distinctness, the override-mismatch validator, the
      reconcile already-present map, the save-path DefiningColOwner
      (an inactive node owns no defining col), and the maybe-placed
      id_opt/error_label.
    - heralds: the rule table emits bare '(inactiveNode)'; updated the
      elisp fixture 'tests/elisp/herald-rules.sexp' to match.
    Tests updated: uninterpreted2 (asserts anonymous parse + leniency),
    deleted the obsolete negative-membership extraction test,
    source_switch + update_buffer_util unit tests, and repurposed the
    duplicate-inactive validation test to assert NO collision (an
    inactive placeholder is not a content member). Full suite green
    (442).

** Test-harness note
   'tests/unit/*.rs' files are '#[path]'-included into the lib's
   'cfg(test)' modules, so they compile only under a bare
   'cargo nextest run' (no '--test' filter) -- the per-target runs
   ('--test grouped_*') skip them. Always run the FULL suite (443
   tests) before declaring a step green.
