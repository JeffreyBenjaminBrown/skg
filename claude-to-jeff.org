* Why skg-id-stack-mode appeared in M-x

'define-minor-mode' always creates an interactive command — that's
how Emacs minor modes work (users toggle them with M-x). There's no
built-in way to make a minor mode non-interactive at definition time.

Fixed by adding a completion-predicate after the definition:

  (put 'skg-id-stack-mode 'completion-predicate #'ignore)

This tells Emacs's completion machinery to exclude it from M-x
results, while still letting 'skg-edit-id-stack' activate it
programmatically.
