that you don't see how to do, or not without doing something ugly.

Note that the reasons to stop do not include deviations from the plan on
*how* to achieve the plan's goals.
Those deviations should be recorded, in progress.org
(leave the original plan as is)
but -- because I trust you -- do not constitute reason to stop.

When you do stop, report what's new in progress.org,
divided into these categories:
- 'what's done and went well',
- "Changes to the plan's envisioned order."
- 'what's imperfect about what's done,"
  Don't include things just because they are missing now but part of the plan.
- "choices determining the future"

If you have no questions for me, do not stop. Keep your report in progress.org,
but then return to executing more of the plan.

Even if you do have questions for me,
after writing them, you might decide that they are trivial.
Do not stop in that case, either.
If you are uncertain of the answer to a question,
but the choice would not be hard to reverse later,
then just go with what seems best to you.
