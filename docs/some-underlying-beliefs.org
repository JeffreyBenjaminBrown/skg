* why Skg
Skg is the result of a few beliefs about knowledge:

- [[./docs/wise-people-say-write.md][Writing is powerful.]] It can -- like schooling, meditation, or therapy -- radically help a person.
- [[./docs/sharing-is-powerful.md][Even at small scales, sharing is powerful.]] There are people to learn from in every domain.
- The freedom to think and write privately is important.
- The freedom to divide, join, connect and traverse ideas is important.
- Reading can, and sometimes should, be like meditating. Too much novelty wastes a better opportunity. We should collect, arrange, and traverse that which we most value, like how the ancients lovingly memorized poems.
- Writing and reading are intertwined. A writer can usefully suggest options to a reader (likely the writer's future self), and a reader can navigate more consciously than typical linear reading envisions.
